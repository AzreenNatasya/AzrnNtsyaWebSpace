Internship Highlights

Facilities Tasks
At the ground floor, a receipt needs to be made for both deposit and final bill. When a patient pays a deposit or makes an initial payment for medical services, it serves as proof. This payment usually guarantees a future appointment or pays for initial costs. After all medical services have been delivered, the patient receives a detailed summary called the final bill. It lists all of the expenses paid for the course of treatment, including any modifications for deposits or insurance coverage.
Scanning is a process of converting paper-based documents into digital formats. This is a key step in the management of patient records. With digital records, accessing and sharing patient information becomes quicker, more reliable, and more secure. The ability to scan and print documents effectively also ensures that important patient records are maintained properly for legal and medical purposes.
The trolley is used to collect medical records from other departments so that the medical report department can proceed with coding, reporting, indexing, and tracking processes of the records.
The IVF records include patient history, diagnostic results, treatment protocols, medication details, embryology data, and outcome reports. During my internship, I was responsible for managing and storing IVF records, which also enabled data analysis for research and quality improvement in fertility care.
